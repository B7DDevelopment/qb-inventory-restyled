# OL Inspired Inventory

**`Overview`
A fully custom-built, high-performance inventory UI for QBCore FiveM servers. Designed from scratch with a sleek dark theme, smooth animations, and a modern layout that puts player experience first.

`Features`
Compact Collapsible Layout — Inventory sits centered on the left side of the screen. The 5-slot hotbar is always visible. Press the expand button and the full inventory slides upward with a smooth animation, revealing 30 scrollable slots in a clean 5-column grid. Collapse it and it snaps back to center. Your gameplay view stays clear.

`Tab Toggle`  — Open and close your inventory with a single Tab press. No fumbling with keybinds.

`Custom Slot Design`  — Every slot features a diagonal gradient background with a hand-crafted SVG crescent swoosh in the bottom-left corner. Cyan diamond slot markers sit on the top-right corner with the slot number inside. The look is clean, dark, and unmistakably premium.

`Smart Tooltips`  — Hover over any item to see its name, weight, and description in a compact tooltip. A "Details" button with a mouse icon sits in the header for quick access. No oversized image panels taking up space.

`Right-Click Context Menu`  — Right-click any item to see a redesigned popup showing the item image, name, weight, and description at the top, with Use and Give action buttons at the bottom. Clean split layout, no clutter.

`SVG Quality Ring`  — Items with durability show a circular progress ring in the bottom-left corner of the slot. The ring drains as quality drops and changes color: green above 50%, amber between 25-50%, red below 25%. All rendered as inline SVG for crisp display at any resolution.

`C-Bracket Weight Indicator`  — The player header features a 270-degree arc (shaped like an open parenthesis) that fills with cyan as your carry weight increases. Turns orange at 60% and red at 80%.

`Colored Item Notifications`  — When you receive or lose an item, a compact notification card slides in from the right side. Green left border for received items, red for removed. Fades out after 3 seconds.

`Second Container Support`  — Open a trunk, stash, or any storage and it appears directly below your inventory with its own styled header bar showing a warehouse icon, weight bracket, container name, and weight display. Both grids show 3 rows at a time with smooth scrolling.

`Player Info Bar`  — A slim bar below the header shows your citizen ID, name, job, and cash balance with subtle icon separators. Everything at a glance without taking up screen space.

`Search` — Click the search icon to reveal an inline search bar that filters your inventory slots in real-time by item name.**
